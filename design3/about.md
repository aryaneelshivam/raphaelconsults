## About Us

### Who We Are
Raphael Travel & Educational Consults is an international education consultancy with a presence in **Zimbabwe (Southern Africa), Ghana (West Africa), and India (Asia)**.  

We serve as a trusted gateway to global education, guiding students with unbiased, smooth, and professional services. Our aim is to help students reach new heights by offering access to comprehensive courses and institutions in their preferred study destinations.

We strive to build a strong network of experienced counsellors who understand each student’s goals and guide them constructively. Our vision is to become your preferred partner in helping you gain the wings to soar high in life.

Our services are carefully tailored to meet the academic and travel needs of ambitious youth seeking a successful future.
